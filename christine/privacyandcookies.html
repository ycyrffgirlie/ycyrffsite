What are cookies?

Cookies are text files stored on your computer that are sent from the site. The information contains in a cookie could be which site set the cookie, how long the cookie lasts.

How does this site used cookie?

I use a cookie for the Hangman game and some of the above services do. The Hangman game cookie that is set is used to hold the current word. The third parties cookies that are set are either for visitor analysis or for social media purposes.

Managing Cookies

You can delete cookies and you can also set your browser to reject cookies.

Clearing cookies:

Below are the instructions of how to clear cookies depening on your browsers.

  •  Internet Explorer

    Because differnt versions of Internet Explorer have differnt methods of deleting cookies, I am refering you to Microsoft's Help which list the differnt methods used by the various versions (IE 6 - IE 9).

    •  Chrome

    1. Click the wrench icon on the browser toolbar.
    2. Select Settings.
    3. Click Show advanced settings.
    4. In the "Privacy" section, click the Content settings button.
    5. In the "Cookies" section, you can either:
      • Delete all cookies, click Remove all at the bottom of the dialog.
      • Delete a specific cookie, select the site that issued the cookie, then the cookie, and click Remove.

    Taken from Google Help

     Firefox
    Delete all cookies

    1. At the top of the Firefox window, click on the Tools menu, and select Clear Recent History....
    2. Set Time range to clear to Everything.
    3. Click on the arrow next to Details to expand the list of history items.
    4. Select Cookies and make sure that other items you want to keep are not selected.
    5. Click Clear Now to clear the cookies and close the Clear Recent History window.

    Delete cookies for a single site

    1. At the top of the Firefox window, click on the Edit menu and select Preferences
    2. Select the Privacy panel.
    3. Set Firefox will: to Use custom settings for history.
    4. Click Show Cookies.... The Cookies window will appear.
    5. In the Search: field, type the name of the site whose cookies you want to remove. The cookies that match your search will be displayed.
    6. Select the cookie(s) in the list to remove and click Remove Cookie.
      • Select the first cookie and press Shift+End to select all the cookies in the list.
    7. Click Close to close the Cookies window.

    Taken from Mozilla Support

  •  Opera

    Settings > Preferences > Advanced > Cookies

    Delete new cookies when exiting Opera

    Some websites require you to accept cookies to use their services. If you want these sites to work, but you do not want to keep cookies between visits, select "Delete new cookies when exiting Opera".

    Taken from Opera Help

  •  Safari

    1. Choose Safari > Preferences, and then click Privacy.
    2. Click Details.
    3. Select one or more website that stores cookies, and then click Remove or Remove All.
    4. When you finish removing websites, click Done.

    Taken from Apple Support

  •  Konqueror
    1. Open Konqueror and select Settings from menu bar
    2. Click on Configure Konqueror
    3. Select Cookies in the Configure window
    4. Select Management tab
    5. Either:
      • Select the cookie you wish to delete and click on Delete
      • Click on Delete All to delete all cookies
